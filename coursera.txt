Coursera is an American online learning platform founded by Stanford professors Andrew Ng and Daphne Kollerthat offers massive open online courses (MOOC), specializations, and degrees.
Coursera works with universities and other organizations to offer online courses, specializations, and degrees in a variety of subjects, such as engineering, humanities, medicine, biology, social sciences, mathematics, business, computer science, digital marketing, data science, and others.
As of June 2018, Coursera had more than 33 million registered usersand more than 2,400 courses. As of August 2019 the number of course offerings was 3,600.
